Live gently. Grow together.

EcoVillage is a small, eco-friendly village that blends regenerative agriculture, renewable energy and cooperative living to create resilient neighbourhoods.

Regenerative Farms

Food forests, permaculture gardens and community orchards that feed the neighbourhood.

Shared Renewable Energy

Solar microgrids and smart storage reduce energy costs and keep the lights on when needed.

Circular Systems

Composting toilets, greywater recycling, and local materials reduce waste and emissions.

Village life

Snapshots of daily life — from shared meals to cooperative workshops.
